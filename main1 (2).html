why choose us?

Flowers are important not just for the aesthetics, adornment, or preparation of Gajra, Garland, Veni, or Bouquets, but also for their industrial value. Some flowers, such as roses, Jasmines, tuberose, Kevda, and Bakul, are used to extract essential oils, which are used to make perfumes, fragrances, and attars.

Commercial floriculture is becoming important from the export angle. The liberalization of industrial and trade policies paved the way for development of export oriented production of cut flowers.
